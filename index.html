The System

How VineCare Works

Three quiet, powerful layers that make sure no one goes unanswered.

Step 01

Automatic Acknowledgment

The moment someone reaches out — by SMS, WhatsApp, or web form — VineCare sends a warm, personalized response in under 30 seconds. No one is left wondering if anyone heard them.

Step 02

Smart Handoff

VineCare identifies when a conversation needs a human touch — grief, crisis, major giving intent — and alerts the right leader immediately. AI handles the routine; humans handle the sacred.

Step 03

Stewardship Tracking

Every interaction is logged, tagged, and tracked in a clean dashboard. Follow-up reminders, engagement history, and care notes — so no one ever falls through the cracks again.
